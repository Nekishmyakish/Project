Why Learn.

Learn UI Design student reached out to me about how he might redesign

nd I ended up doing an hour-long call with some other students where we
talked about techniques and tips for redesigning his site.

We covered a bunch of strategies while doing a make-shift redesign, and I want to talk
about some of them today: a nutshell, the Squint Test means that when you squint,
you should see the most important stuff first, the second-mostmportant stuff second,
the third-most important stuff third,

And so on. If you squint at your design and the first thing you see is a secondary
sidebar that’s only useful in very limited cases… that’s bad news.
